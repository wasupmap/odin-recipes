Mos Burger Inspired Chicken Teriyaki Burger

Description

As always, just use your cook-senses for the most part. Cook everything to taste. There's no need for a measuring cup or spoons. Go with your gut feeling, mate!

Ingredients

Teriyaki sauce

  • 1L of sake
  • 1L of soy sauce
  • 1L of mirin

The rest

  • Brioche buns fresh from an oven
  • Chicken thighs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh lettuce
  • Fresh tamatoes
  • Mayonaise

Steps

Teriyaki sauce

  1. Mix all three ingredients in a pot in equal portion
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Stir while the mixture is at a rolling boil until desired consistency

The burger

  1. Remove the bones from the chicken thigh
  2. Fry the chicken thigh until internal temp. reaches 74 degrees celsius
  3. Flame the chicken thighs with a torch until they develop a lovely crust
  4. Assemble the burger to your liking. Ain't nobody need to tell you how to assemble a burger, comeon!
  5. Make sure you don't forget to top it off with the teriyaki sauce you stood there stirring away patiently until it reached your desired consistency to craft
