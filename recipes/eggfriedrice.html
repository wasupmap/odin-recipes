Classic Egg Fried Rice

Description

Fuiyo! As uncle Roger would say, a little MSG can't hurt. We only need 5 ingredients to perfect this Egg Fried Rice.

Ingredients

  • 1-day-old cold cooked white rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • MSG
  • White pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a cup of vegetable oil in a flaming hot wok. Make sure there's wok hay
  2. Beat a few eggs in a bowl until it reaches a gooey cosistency
  3. Throw the beaten eggs in the flaming hot wok
  4. Work the beaten eggs in the wok hard with some good ol' spatula
  5. Once eggs are 85% cooked, throw in the 1 day old cold cooked rice into the flaming hot wok
  6. Work that wok hard over high flames
  7. Add a bucket of MSG
  8. Add some white pepper to taste
