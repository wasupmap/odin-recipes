Mole con pollo

Description

This rich, chocolatey traditional Mexican dish composes of dozens of ingredients. It takes decades of experience to perfect if cooked from scratch. Today, we learn the easy way with pre-made mole paste. It will still be delicious!

Ingredients

  • Bone-in chicken thighs or drumsticks (as many as you like)
  • Oil (olive oil, motor oil, avocado oil, coconut oil, or whatever you like)
  • Knorr chicken cubes for that savoury umami
  • Organic european butter (the more european the better)
  • Sesame seeds
  • Pomegranate
  • Most importantly:Doña María Mole
  • Chicken broth
  • Abuelita chocolate
  • A little MSG can't hurt anybody
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the raw chicken with MSG, salt, pepper, garlic powder, european butter, and oil of your choice
  2. Bag the chicken in a safe sous-vide BPA-free bag
  3. Sous-vide the chicken at 75 degrees celcius for 2 hours
  4. Flame the chicken with a torch until crispy
  5. In a large pot:
    1. Fill with chicken broth (enough to cover the chicken)
    2. Throw in Knorr chicken cubes
    3. Bring to a rolling boil
    4. Throw in the Abuelita Chocolate (as much as you want, make sure to taste it)
    5. Throw in the Doña María Mole (empty the damn bottle)
    6. Add a generous sprinkle of MSG
    7. Throw in a cube of European butter, why not
    8. Add oil in an amount that would satisfy your soul... fat is flavour
    9. Keep stirring until the consistency is as thick as you like it
  6. Now, plate the chicken with steamed white rice and tortillas on the side
  7. Cover the chicken with a thick blanket of mole we just made in the pot
  8. Sprinkle pomegranate and sesame seeds on top
  9. Enjoy before it gets cold!
